Our Programs

Flag Football

Perfect for young athletes ages 5-14 who are starting their football journey. Flag football is a fast-paced, non-contact sport that builds skills, teamwork, and confidence.

What to Expect:

  • Fun, age-appropriate competitive leagues
  • Professional coaching and instruction
  • Focus on skill development and teamwork
  • No tackling - safe and accessible
  • Weekly games and practices

Tackle Football

EPW's tackle football program combines competitive play with a strong focus on safety, discipline, and teamwork. With over 50 years of tradition, we've developed champions who go on to play in high school and beyond.

What to Expect:

  • Competitive division leagues for all skill levels
  • Experienced coaches with CPR and safety certification
  • State-of-the-art equipment and training facilities
  • Character education and academic focus
  • Spring and Fall seasons

Cheerleading

Join the energy and excitement of EPW Cheer! Our cheerleading program welcomes young athletes ages 5-14 to learn cheer, dance, and teamwork in a fun and supportive environment.

What to Expect:

  • Professional cheerleading instruction
  • Learn stunts, pyramids, and routines
  • Perform at games and community events
  • Build confidence and school spirit
  • Strong emphasis on safety and technique
