USA Football & Safety Commitment

At Escondido Pop Warner, your child's safety is our top priority. We are proud to follow the highest national standards for player safety by partnering with USA Football.

USA Football Certified Coaches

All of our coaches are USA Football Certified and have completed the Heads Up Football training program. This ensures every coach is equipped with the tools and knowledge to teach the game safely and responsibly.

Our Coaches are Trained in:

  • Concussion Recognition and Response - Identify and properly respond to head injuries
  • Heat Preparedness and Hydration - Prevent heat-related illnesses
  • Proper Equipment Fitting - Ensure protective equipment is correctly sized and worn
  • Safer Blocking and Tackling Techniques - Teach age-appropriate techniques that minimize injury risk
  • Emergency Action Planning - Prepared for medical emergencies with quick response protocols

Concussion Information

Concussions are a serious injury that can occur in football. EPW is committed to recognizing and responding appropriately to all potential concussions.

What is a Concussion?

A concussion is a type of traumatic brain injury caused by a blow to the head or rapid acceleration/deceleration of the brain. It can affect how the brain works.

Common Symptoms:

  • Headache or pressure in head
  • Confusion or dizziness
  • Memory problems
  • Balance issues
  • Sensitivity to light/noise
  • Nausea or vomiting

EPW Response Protocol

If a concussion is suspected, immediate action is taken to protect the player.

Our Process:

  • Player removed from activity immediately
  • Medical evaluation on site or referral
  • Parent/guardian notification
  • Physician clearance required before return
  • Gradual return-to-activity protocol
  • Documentation and follow-up
