Escondido Pop Warner Cheer

Cheerleading is a fun and exciting way for kids ages 3 to 13 to learn new skills, build confidence, and make lifelong friends. We offer programs for all experience levels from beginners to advanced athletes with divisions based on age (organized by age on July 31).

What's Included

Our comprehensive cheer programs include everything your child needs to succeed:

  • Complete Uniform Package: Skirt, top, cropped long sleeves, briefs, pom poms, bow, and backpack
  • JAMZ Cheer Camp: One-day professional camp led by expert cheerleaders
  • Team Registration: Covers all Pop Warner competitions throughout the season
  • Game Day Performances: Chants, stunts, and routines at football games
  • Competitive Experience: Local and regional competition opportunities
  • Year-Round Options: Advanced divisions offer year-round training

Note: Cheer shoes (all-white) are not included and must be provided separately.
